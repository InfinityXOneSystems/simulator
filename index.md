## Project Overview

The **Simulator Repository** provides business and technical simulation capabilities for the Infinity X One Systems ecosystem. This system enables scenario modeling, future timeline prediction, risk analysis, and optimization recommendations to support strategic planning and decision-making.

---

## Key Features (Planned)

### Scenario Modeling
Create and execute complex business and technical scenarios to evaluate potential outcomes and strategies.

### Timeline Prediction
Generate multiple future timeline predictions based on current data and trends, supporting strategic planning.

### Risk Analysis
Identify potential risks, bottlenecks, and friction points in business processes and technical systems.

### Optimization Recommendations
AI-powered recommendations for optimizing processes, systems, and strategies based on simulation results.
